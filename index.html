Privacy Policy

How the English Set Go Android app handles information.

Last updated: 14 July 2026

English Set Go ("the app", "we", "us", or "our") is an offline-first English learning application. This policy explains what information is handled when you use the app, including its optional rewarded-ad and email-support features.

Learning data stored on your device

Lesson progress, practice scores, Learning Stars, and app preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not operate user accounts and do not upload this learning data to our own servers. You can remove local data by clearing the app's storage in Android settings or uninstalling the app.

Optional advertising and Google AdMob

The app contains an optional rewarded-ad feature provided by Google AdMob through the Google Mobile Ads SDK. Lessons and practice do not require watching ads.

When the advertising feature is used, the Google Mobile Ads SDK may automatically collect and share:

  • IP address information, which may be used to estimate general location;
  • app and ad interactions, such as app launches, taps, and video views;
  • diagnostic information, such as app or SDK performance; and
  • device or account identifiers, including the Android advertising ID and app set ID where available.

This information may be used for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention. Google states that this data is encrypted in transit. Learn more in Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses information for advertising.

Consent and advertising choices

Where required, Google's consent flow is requested before an ad is loaded. You can continue using all core learning features without watching an ad. You can also manage or reset advertising identifiers and related choices through your Android device settings.

Email support

Feedback, suggestion, and support buttons open your device's email application. We receive only the information you choose to include in an email. Support messages are used to respond to your request and are retained only as reasonably necessary for support, legal, fraud-prevention, or security purposes.

Data sharing and sale

We do not sell personal information. Advertising-related information may be shared with Google as described above. Information sent voluntarily by email is handled by the relevant email providers and by us for support.

Data retention and deletion

Local learning data remains on your device until you clear app storage or uninstall the app. For deletion of information you voluntarily sent to our support email, contact us using the address below. Google retains advertising-related information according to its own policies and user controls.

Children

English Set Go is intended for a general audience aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. If you believe a child has sent personal information to our support email, contact us so we can delete it.

Security

The app does not transmit local lesson progress to our servers. Google reports that information handled by the Google Mobile Ads SDK is encrypted in transit using TLS. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, but we take reasonable steps to limit the information handled by the app.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when app features, legal requirements, or third-party services change. The updated date at the top will identify the latest version.

Contact

For privacy questions, support, or deletion requests relating to information sent to us, email edev.suraj@gmail.com.

The learning experience remains available offline. Internet access is used only for optional online services such as rewarded ads and email support.
